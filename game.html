War of the Worm

Game
Over

Controls:

Player 1 - The Bird:


WASD: Move
E: Shoot Egg

Player 2 - The Mole:


Arrow Keys: Move
0 (numpad): Shoot Mud
(Turn on num lock!)

How to Play:


Both players are racing to grab the worm in the centre and return to their home with it in their mouth so they can savour the meal. They must be careful to avoid the spikes which will stun them and make them drop the worm!

Each player has a trick up their sleeve to hinder their opponent: They can shoot a projectile vertically towards the other player that knocks the worm out of them and leaves their head spinning! Who will be early enough to get the worm, and who will be left eating the dust?
