A little bit about myself:

I am a developer of websites, VR, and games. I mostly use C# and Unity, and created a selection of educational VR games and experiences as an apprentice at Sophia Technologies Ltd. Right now I am working on obtaining a degree in Computer Science.

Why Web Development?

I want to pursue a career in Web Development because I feel that websites are a crucial backbone of our lives and our businesses; They embody human thoughts and ideas in a visual and permanent way. I love problem solving through writing code, and I love seeing the effects of my work visually and instantly so I can continually tweak and improve my work to make it the best possible version it can be.

Why am I applying for the Founders and Coders course?

I want to attend the Founders and Coders Software Foundation course because I am enthusiastic about broadening my knowledge in the field of web development, both front-end and back-end, and having the opportunity to study DevOps and AI engineering would be very enjoyable and a springboard for preparing myself to contribute to the World Wide Web as a professional Web Developer.
